Emerald Knights: Livingston High School
FTC Robotics Team

Our Mission

Our goals are to improve our building, planning and organization skills, as well as to inspire and spread STEM programs in our community. We believe that robotics competitions are not a contest against other teams, but rather an opportunity to better and improve our own skills and work ethic.

The Team

Lead by co-captains Andie Weiser-Schlesinger and Dmitry Sambuk, the Livingston Emerald Knights Robotics Team was founded in 2015 and started with just three members. Two years after our establishment, we have managed to grow to over 40 dedicated members.

Coding

Building

Our build team uses metal parts, sensors, and motors to make a purposeful, working robot. In addition, they wire the robot and make models of the robot with an AutoCAD program.
